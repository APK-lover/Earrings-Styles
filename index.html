Care Tips for Your Gold Plated Earrings

To keep your gold plated earrings looking new, follow these simple tips:

  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, and lotions to prevent tarnishing.
  • Store your earrings in a soft cloth pouch or jewelry box when not in use.
  • Clean gently with a soft, dry cloth to maintain their shine.
  • Wear them last after applying makeup or hair products.
